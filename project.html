Crop Sense - The Crop Recommendation System

Introducing "Crop Sense" - our major college project that is transforming crop decision-making for millions of Indian farmers through precision agriculture. Utilizing a robust soil database and expert recommendations, we optimize crop choices, resulting in increased productivity and reduced losses. Our ensemble model with 95% accuracy enables precise yield prediction, empowering farmers to make informed decisions and mitigate risks. With a focus on sustainable practices, we contribute to food security, water conservation, and economic growth. "Crop Sense" showcases our expertise in data analysis and innovative crop selection methods.
